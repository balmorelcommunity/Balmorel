dk-highspatialres

The dk-highspatialres branch is used for analysing the effect of various spatial resolutions in Denmark. The poster below illustrates a case study for the EGU 2024 conference, with source codes under the EGU 2024 Poster tag in all mentioned repositories here.

The pre-processing of raw data is performed using the following balmorel-preprocessing tool, and the prepared .inc-files for the fully resolved model can be found in the data repository.
