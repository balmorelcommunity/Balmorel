The data format of Balmorel is .inc-files. There are different approaches to pre-processing, the simplest being creating a new .inc-file through GAMS Studio. xls2gams Can be used to produce .inc-files from excel sheets, and for python we recommend the IncFile class from our pybalmorel package.
